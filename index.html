About

Lazenby Coin Facts provides accurate information on coin mintages and values for German, American, and English (coming soon) coins


This website provides value information for coins based on a condition of "Fine". For non-coin collectors,
"fine" can best be desribed as moderate to considerable wear, the design aspects and words of the coin should be visible
but lacking in detail. When in doubt of a coin's grade be sure to visit a trusted local coin dealer to learn more!


About the Author

The website author, Joshua Lazenby, has been a numismatist for almost a decade. He is a recipient of the South Carolina
Numismatics Association's Young Numismatist of the Year award, and has attended two American Numismatics Association Summer Seminars.
Joshua is currently assembling a full circulation set of the coins of the German Empire.
